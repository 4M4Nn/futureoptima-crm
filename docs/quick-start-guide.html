Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with your new AI-powered CRM in 5 simple steps

1
🔐

Login to Your CRM

Open your browser and go to your CRM URL. Use the admin credentials below to sign in. You'll see the dashboard with live stats immediately.

→Visit: https://futureoptima-crm.vercel.app
→Email: admin@futureoptima.in
→Password: FutureOptima@2025
→Click "Sign In" — you're in!
Tip: Change your password immediately in Settings → My Profile → Change Password.
2
👤

Add Your First Lead

When a student walks in or calls, add them as a lead immediately. The AI will automatically score them as Hot/Warm/Cold within seconds.

→Click Leads in the sidebar
→Click the + Add Lead button (top right)
→Fill: Full Name, Phone Number, Lead Source, Course Interest
→Click + Add Lead — AI scores it automatically!
→Click the lead name to see the full profile and AI analysis
Tip: Click Score All to re-score all existing leads at once. Hot leads = call immediately!
3
🎓

Enroll a Student

When a lead decides to join, convert them to a student with a course enrollment and installment plan — takes 30 seconds.

→Open the lead → Click the gold Enroll button
→Select the Course (fee auto-fills)
→Enter Discount Amount if applicable (scholarship, referral, etc.)
→Select Number of Installments (1 to 12)
→See the Net Fee calculated live → Click Confirm Enrollment
→Student appears in the Students section automatically!
Tip: The installment due dates are auto-created monthly from enrollment date. You can see them in Student Detail.
4
💰

Record a Payment & Get Receipt

When a student pays, record it in seconds and instantly download a professional PDF receipt with QR code verification.

→Click Payments → Record Payment
→Type student's phone number → Click Search
→See student details and click the installment to select it (amount auto-fills)
→Choose payment method (Cash/UPI/Bank/Cheque) and enter reference
→Click Record Payment → Click green Download Receipt PDF
→Print and give to the student. Done! ✅
Tip: You can also download receipts from Student Detail page → Payment History tab (Download button on each payment).
5
📊

Check Your Reports

Every month, check your reports to track fee collection, course performance, and counselor conversion rates.

→Click Reports in the sidebar
→Fee Collection: Pick a month → see daily chart and total collected
→Course Enrollment: See which courses are most popular
→Lead Sources: See where leads are coming from (Facebook, walk-in, etc.)
→Counselor Performance: See team conversion rates (Admin only)
→Click Export CSV on any tab to download for Excel
Tip: Also check Meta Ads page to connect your Facebook & Instagram ads for automatic lead import.

Project Overview

Complete AI-Powered Institute Management System

Future Optima CRM is a custom-built, AI-powered Customer Relationship Management system designed exclusively for Future Optima IT Solutions. It automates lead management, student enrollment, fee collection, and business reporting — all in one unified platform.

The system uses local AI (Ollama with llama3.2) for zero-cost lead scoring, generates professional PDF receipts instantly, and integrates with Facebook and Instagram to automatically capture ad leads.
